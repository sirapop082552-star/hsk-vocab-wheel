The plot
Part 02 — Riley's story
🎤 คิตตี้ opens · then มงกี้ → อันอัน → เอม → น้ำมนต์ → โม

Five emotions, one girl

Moving day 📦

Riley is an 11-year-old girl who moves from Minnesota to San Francisco because of her father's new job. Inside her mind, five emotions control her feelings and decisions.

— มงกี้

Lost in the mind 🌀

Joy wants Riley to be happy all the time. One day, Joy and Sadness accidentally leave the control centre.

— อันอัน

Everything falls apart 😟

Only Anger, Fear, and Disgust are left in control. Riley becomes upset and decides to run away.

— เอม

Joy's lesson 💡

Joy learns that sadness is important because it helps people understand and support each other. Joy lets Sadness take control.

— น้ำมนต์

Coming home 🏠

Riley goes back home and tells her parents how she feels. They comfort her, and she slowly accepts her new life.

— โม
Main characters
Part 03 — The voices at the console

Meet the five emotions (and Riley)

⭐

Joy

The sparkling leader of the team. She wants Riley to be happy every single day — until she learns that happiness is not everything.

💧

Sadness

Quiet, gentle, and misunderstood. By the end of the story she turns out to be the most important emotion of all.

🔥

Anger

Short, red, and always ready to explode — literally. He fights for what is fair for Riley, with a head full of fire.

😨

Fear

Riley's safety inspector. He checks every possible danger — and counts surviving a school day as a great success.

🥦

Disgust

Stylish and honest. She protects Riley from being poisoned — physically and socially. Her greatest enemy: broccoli.

🎀

Riley

The 11-year-old girl whose mind we live in. Moving to a new city turns her whole world — inside and out — upside down.

What we learned
Part 05 — Lessons from the movie

Every emotion has a job to do

🌈

Every emotion matters

Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear and Disgust all protect Riley in their own way. We need all of them — not just happiness.

💙

It's okay to be sad

Sadness helps us slow down, understand ourselves, and lets other people know we need support. Crying is not weakness.

🗣️

Talk about your feelings

Riley only feels better after she tells her parents the truth. Sharing feelings honestly is how people help each other.

🌱

Growing up is hard — and normal

New city, new school, new life. Change is difficult for everyone, and adjusting takes time. That's completely okay.
