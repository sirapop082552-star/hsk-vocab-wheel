From a small novel to a global legend

In 1997, J.K. Rowling published her first novel — an idea she famously began writing on a delayed train. Warner Bros. bought the film rights, and director Chris Columbus turned it into the movie that opened the whole saga.

Part 02 — Plot summary (Ⅰ of Ⅱ)
🎤 Speaker 1 of 2

The Boy Who Lived

Harry Potter is an orphan raised by his cruel aunt and uncle, the Dursleys, who make him sleep in a cupboard under the stairs. On his 11th birthday, a giant named Hagrid arrives with a letter — and a secret: “You're a wizard, Harry.”

Harry discovers he is famous in the wizarding world as “The Boy Who Lived” — the only person to survive the curse of the dark lord Voldemort. He buys his wand in Diagon Alley, runs through the wall at Platform 9¾, and rides the Hogwarts Express to his new school, where he becomes best friends with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.

Part 02 — Plot summary (Ⅱ of Ⅱ)
🎤 Speaker 2 of 2

The secret of the Stone

At Hogwarts, the three friends discover that something precious is hidden beneath the school: the Philosopher's Stone, a magical stone that can give eternal life. They believe someone is trying to steal it for Voldemort, who survives as a spirit without a body.

To protect the Stone they pass deadly trials — the Devil's Snare, a room of flying keys, and a giant chess game where Ron sacrifices himself so his friends can go on. In the final chamber, Harry faces Professor Quirrell, possessed by Voldemort himself.

Harry wins — not with a spell, but because his mother's love still protects him. The Stone is destroyed, and Voldemort's shadow escapes… for now.

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

On their first night, first-year students cross the Black Lake by boat — towards the castle that will be their home for seven years.

Part 05 — Lessons, quotes, and new words
🎤 1 speaker

What this film teaches us

🤝

Friendship wins

Harry never passes the final trials alone — every victory comes from the different talents of his friends combined.

🦁

Real courage

Courage is not the absence of fear — it is doing the right thing while you are afraid, even standing up to your friends.

❤️

Love is the strongest magic

A mother's sacrifice protects Harry better than any spell — love is the one power evil cannot touch.

🧭

Our choices define us

The Sorting Hat almost puts Harry in Slytherin — but what we choose shows who we truly are.

⭐ Quotes we love

  • “It does not do to dwell on dreams and forget to live.” — Dumbledore
  • “It takes a great deal of bravery to stand up to our enemies, but just as much to stand up to our friends.” — Dumbledore
  • “You're a wizard, Harry.” — Hagrid

📖 New words & phrases

orphan — a child whose parents have died
wand — a magic stick used to cast spells
spell / curse — magic words (good / evil)
potion — a magical drink
scar — a mark left on skin by a wound
invisible — impossible to see
sacrifice — giving up something for others
“stand up to (someone)” — to bravely face or resist someone
